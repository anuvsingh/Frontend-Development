Clow Council

The Clow Council is the elite feline division of The Whisker Syndicate, tasked with maintaining order in their secretive world. Led by the cunning and fearless tabby, Shadowclaw, the team includes Whisker, the tech-savvy Siamese, and Ember, the agile Bengal known for her swift moves. Their mission: to outsmart and capture the rogue Mouseketeer Trio, who have stolen vital Syndicate blueprints. With razor-sharp instincts and unmatched strategy, the cats use stealth and precision to track the mice. For Shadowclaw and her team, failure is not an option—the reputation of the Syndicate and the balance of power are at stake.

Cheese Chasers

The Cheese Chasers is a renegade faction of The Whisker Syndicate, determined to expose the Syndicate’s secrets for the good of all rodents. Led by Squeaky, the brains of the operation, the team includes Nibbles, the daring infiltrator, and Dash, the speedster who never gets caught. Their mission is to evade capture while spreading stolen secrets that reveal feline plans to suppress mice everywhere. Using their wit, speed, and tiny size to outmaneuver their feline pursuers, the Mouseketeers turn every corner into an escape route. For them, freedom for mice everywhere depends on their ability to stay one step ahead.
